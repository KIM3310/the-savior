# Monetization Playbook - the-savior

This file is a commercial planning artifact, not a guarantee of revenue. Use it to make the repository legible as a sellable product, pilot, workshop, license, or proof-of-value package.

## Commercial Lane

- **Lane:** B2C wellness and ads funnel
- **Primary buyer:** Wellness creators, small communities, and users seeking low-friction reflection tools.
- **Revenue wedge:** Cloudflare Pages mindfulness app with journaling, coaching, fallbacks, ads, and mobile packaging path.

## Offer Ladder

1. **Freemium wellness app** - pricing hypothesis: Ads + consent
2. **Paid guided packs** - pricing hypothesis: $4-$12 paid packs
3. **Creator/community templates** - pricing hypothesis: $5-$10/month sync/community

## Acquisition Channels

- SEO/reflection content
- Creator partnerships
- Short calming routine clips

## Proof Gates Before Selling

- npm run verify passes
- Fallback works without keys
- Privacy boundary is visible

## Risk Boundaries

- Not clinical care
- Sensitive content privacy
- Escalation/safety copy required

## 30/60/90 Day GTM

- **30 days:** Publish 20 guided routines
- **60 days:** Add creator pack page
- **90 days:** Test ads and paid pack

## Metrics That Matter

- Routine completion
- Pack conversion
- Retention

## Positioning Note

Lead with the specific operational pain, then show the runnable proof, then offer a narrow pilot. Do not pitch the repository as a vague app; pitch it as a concrete workflow with evidence, risk boundaries, and a next buying step.
